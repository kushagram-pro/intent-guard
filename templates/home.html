Why it exists

Clear instructions matter in financial systems

Trading systems can move fast, but vague instructions create expensive mistakes. This project makes sure requests are explicit, measurable, and checked before they are routed anywhere.

Decision states

  • ALLOW when the instruction is explicit and safe.
  • ASK when more detail or confirmation is needed.
  • BLOCK when the trade violates safety rules.
  • PARTIAL when a mixed instruction needs selective execution.
