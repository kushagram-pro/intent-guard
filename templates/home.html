What this project is trying to solve

Trading agents move fast. Intent Guard slows down the dangerous part: it turns fuzzy human instructions into structured intents, checks policy and risk, and only then lets a simulated execution layer continue.

Core decisions

  • ALLOW when the instruction is explicit and safe.
  • ASK when more detail or confirmation is needed.
  • BLOCK when the trade violates safety rules.
  • PARTIAL when a mixed instruction needs selective execution.
